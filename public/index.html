Expert Tips

Guidance for Board Exams

Strategic tips and structured guidance to help students prepare smarter for CBSE and ICSE board examinations.

01

Know the Exam Pattern

Understand mark distribution, question types (MCQ, short answer, long answer), and time allocation for each subject before you start preparing.

  • Download the official marking scheme
  • Identify high-weightage chapters
  • Practice previous year papers
02

Smart Revision Strategy

Revision is more important than re-reading. Use active recall, spaced repetition, and chapter summaries to retain what you've studied.

  • Make concise notes per chapter
  • Revise a chapter within 24 hours
  • Use our summary sheets to revise fast
03

Time Management in Exams

Allocate time per section, don't get stuck on one question, and always leave 10 minutes for revision at the end of the exam.

  • Attempt easy questions first
  • Allocate marks ÷ 1.5 = minutes per question
  • Practice with timed mock tests
